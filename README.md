# Privacy Policy for Staying Alive: Safety Check

**Last updated: January 12, 2026**

## 1. Introduction
Staying Alive ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our iOS application (the "App") collects, uses, and discloses your information.

**Important Disclaimer:** This App is a secondary safety tool and is **NOT a replacement for calling emergency services (such as 911 or 112)**. In an immediate life-threatening situation, always contact local emergency services directly.

## 2. Information We Collect
We prioritize data minimization. We do **NOT** collect your precise location (GPS) data.

### 2.1 Personal Information
To use the App, we collect the minimum information required to send alerts:
*   **Your Name:** Used solely to include in the email alerts sent to your contacts.
*   **Emergency Contact Emails:** The email addresses you designate to receive alerts. We do not use these emails for marketing.

### 2.2 Usage Data
*   **Check-in Timestamps:** We record the time of your check-ins to calculate countdowns.
*   **Device Information:** Limited device identifiers necessary for authentication and notification delivery.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use your information **only to providing the App's functionality**:
*   To track your safety status (Fresh, Safe, Warning, Expired).
*   To send automated email alerts to your designated contacts *only* if you fail to check in.
*   To sync your settings across your devices.

**We do NOT:**
*   Track your location.
*   Sell your personal data to third parties.
*   Read your personal contacts or other private data on your device.

## 4. Data Retention and Security
*   **Retention:** We retain your profile data (Name, Contacts, Check-in history) only as long as your account is active. You may request full data deletion at any time.
*   **Storage:** We use Google Firebase (a secure third-party cloud provider) to store your data.
*   **Security:** We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission and storage.

## 5. Third-Party Services
We use trusted third-party services to support the App's infrastructure:
*   **Google Firebase:** For secure database storage and authentication. [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 6. Your Rights
*   **Access & Update:** You can update all your data directly in the App's "Settings" screen.
*   **Deletion:** You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the support email provided below.

## 7. Children's Privacy
Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

## 9. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
